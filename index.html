About Me

Civil Engineer

Hi, I am Mohammad Mohsin, a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Civil Engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET), where I earned a CGPA of 3.53 out of 4.00. I have consistently pursued academic excellence, achieving a GPA of 5.00 (out of 5) in both my HSC (2019) from Govt. City College and SSC (2017) from Bakolia Govt. High School. Alongside my academic focus, I have developed a strong interest in data science and its applications in civil engineering. My research interests include sustainable construction materials and the use of machine learning to address challenges in civil engineering. I have gained experience in data visualization, statistical analysis, and multidisciplinary research, with a passion for integrating advanced technologies like AI and data science to create innovative, real-world solutions.
